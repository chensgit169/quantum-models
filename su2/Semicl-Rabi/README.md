Calculation of Fouquet quasienergy of semiclassical Rabi model
$$
H(t)=\frac{1}{2}\begin{bmatrix}
 \Delta & g\cos(\omega t)\\
g\cos(\omega t) & -\Delta
\end{bmatrix}
$$
in three parameter regimes:

- weak field: $|g|<\omega$
- small gap: $|\Delta|<\omega$
- slow driving:  $|g|>\omega$, $|\Delta|>\omega$
